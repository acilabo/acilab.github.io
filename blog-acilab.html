Unveiling ACIlab: A Journey into Collective Intelligence

  • Fedelis Zumah
  • Jan 15, 2024
  • 12 Comments

The workshop commenced with a warm welcome, setting the stage for a dynamic exploration of collective intelligence. ACIlab representatives provided an overview of the agenda, building anticipation for an enriching experience. As we delved into the workshop, it was crucial to introduce ACIlab, providing participants with a deeper understanding of our organization, values, mission, vision, and the talented individuals who make up our team.

Presentation of ACIlab.

Welcome to Africa Collective Intelligence Lab, your trusted partner in driving innovation and transformation across the African continent. With operational offices in Ghana, Benin, Nigeria, and Guinea Conakry, ACIlab is an independent consulting company specializing in ideation, sortition, democratic innovations, and organizational transformation. Our mission is to foster the application of collective intelligence principles and techniques across domains, leverage emerging technologies to solve pressing challenges, facilitate inclusive collaboration and promote knowledge sharing, advocate for evidence-based decision-making, drive positive change, and contribute to the betterment of Africa while promoting social inclusion in governance.

In envisioning the future, ACIlab aims to be a leading laboratory for collective intelligence research, driving innovation, collaboration, and problem-solving. Our goal is to make collective intelligence tools universally accessible, using Africa's historical organizational principles and wisdom to unlock the continent's full potential. At ACIlab, collaboration is not just a value; it is the heartbeat of our organization. We believe in the power of collective minds working together, bringing diverse perspectives, skills, and experiences to the table. Through fostering a culture of collaboration, we aim to tackle complex challenges and create a positive ripple effect within our community and beyond.

Innovation is at the core of our identity. Challenges are viewed as opportunities to think creatively and push the boundaries of what is possible. ACIlab is committed to cultivating an environment that nurtures curiosity, experimentation, and the exploration of novel ideas, contributing to positive change in societal issues. Lastly, knowledge is a powerful catalyst for growth and progress. At ACIlab, we embrace the philosophy that knowledge gains its true value when shared. We actively promote the exchange of ideas, insights, and expertise, seeking to empower individuals and communities. Through knowledge sharing, we aim to build a reservoir of wisdom that enriches our organization and the wider network of collaborators, partners, and stakeholders.

Meet Our Team

George Tetteh

Co-founder & Director of Finance and Administration

Bringing over 7 years of experience in Economic Development planning and international consulting, George is a dynamic expert in Financial Economics and Big Data Engineering. His role at ACIlab champions democratic innovations, design thinking, and the integration of cutting-edge digital platforms for groundbreaking ideas.

Arinze Ezirim

Co-founder and Director Communication and Publicity

Arinze specializes in cutting-edge technology solutions, demonstrating a deep understanding of digital technologies and collective intelligence methodologies. With over 4 years in the tech industry, he champions democratic innovations and fosters collaborative thinking.

Fidelis Zumah

Co-founder and Director Research and Innovation

An academic trailblazer, Fidelis holds a Master's degree in Public Health Information and has over 5 years of research and professional experience in the health sector. His role at ACIlab involves integrating collective intelligence into impactful research outcomes.

Bid Oscar

Co-founder and Director Science & Technology

Certified AI Engineer and Full Stack Developer, Bid Oscar brings 3 years of experience as a Data Scientist. His expertise in ML and AI projects, including an AI model for industrial systems diagnosis and solutions to the Multi-Armed Bandit Problem, drives science and technology projects at ACIlab.

Each member of our team contributes a unique set of skills and experiences, collectively driving ACIlab towards its mission and vision.
