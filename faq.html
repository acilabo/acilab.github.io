Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does ACILAB stand for?

    ACILAB stands for Africa Collective Intelligence Lab.

  • Where are your operational offices located?

    Our Headquarters is in Morocco. We also have operational branch offices in Ghana, Benin, Nigeria, and Guinea Conakry.

  • What services does ACILAB provide?

    ACILAB specializes in ideation, democratic innovations, organizational transformation, and more. Explore our "What We Do" section for detailed information.

  • What is ideation, and how does ACILAB approach it?

    Ideation is a creative process fostering innovation. ACILAB employs collaborative thinking and collective intelligence tools to generate and refine groundbreaking ideas. Our approach incorporates Africa's principles for a tailored and holistic process.

  • How does ACILAB champion democratic innovations?

    We integrate democratic principles into decision-making processes, empowering stakeholders to actively participate in shaping communities and organizations. Digital platforms facilitate citizen engagement, participatory budgeting, and deliberative processes.

  • What does ACILAB offer in terms of organizational transformation?

    Our commitment to organizational transformation involves partnering with organizations across sectors to drive meaningful change. We draw inspiration from Africa's rich history of organizational principles to enhance structures, processes, and cultures.

  • How does ACILAB foster collaboration?

    Collaboration is the heartbeat of ACILAB. We bring diverse perspectives, skills, and experiences to the table, tackling complex challenges and driving impactful initiatives. Our culture of collaboration extends to clients, partners, and stakeholders.

  • Who leads ACILAB initiatives?

    ACILAB is expert-led. Our initiatives are driven by a team of seasoned professionals, leaders in their respective fields, ensuring strategies are based on the latest industry insights and best practices.

  • What values guide ACILAB?

    Our values include collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing. We believe in the power of collective minds, fostering a culture of openness, transparency, and continuous learning.

  • How does ACILAB provide client support?

    We prioritize clear communication and responsive support. Our client support team is readily available to address inquiries, concerns, and feedback, ensuring seamless collaboration at every stage.

  • What sets ACILAB apart in providing solutions?

    ACILAB offers tailored solutions crafted to meet the unique requirements of each client. We believe in understanding your organization's goals, challenges, and aspirations to develop customized strategies for success.

  • How can individuals or organizations get involved with ACILAB?

    Contact us through our website or visit our offices to explore collaboration opportunities. We welcome inquiries from those interested in leveraging collective intelligence for positive change.
