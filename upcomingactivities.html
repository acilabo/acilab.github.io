Machine Learning for Collective Intelligence Workshop

  • Bid Oscar
  • Feb 25, 2024 at 10 a.m.
  • 0 Comments

We are thrilled to announce our upcoming workshop on "Machine Learning for Collective Intelligence." This workshop is designed for individuals and teams interested in harnessing the potential of machine learning to enhance collective decision-making and problem-solving.

Date: Feb 25, 2024

Time: 10 a.m.

Location: ABS Building, UM6P Rabat Campus

During this interactive session, you will:

  • Gain insights into the intersection of machine learning and collective intelligence.
  • Explore real-world applications and case studies.
  • Participate in hands-on activities and discussions.
  • Connect with like-minded individuals and experts in the field.

Don't miss this opportunity to expand your knowledge and network in the exciting realm of machine learning and collective intelligence. Reserve your spot now!
